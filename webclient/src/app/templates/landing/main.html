Organize your surfing in a clever way

  • Simple, handy, beautiful

    Urlist is designed to be as easy as possible.
    No tags, no weird options, just links and lists.
    Add notes and customize the cover image to make your lists more personal and beautiful. Browse the links in a snap, using our slideshow feature.

  • The content that matters
    in one short link

    Make a collaborative search with your team at work. Share references on a presentation. Help a friend sharing some good links about a topic you rule. Using Urlist you need only one short link to share a group of links together.

    time you need them

    Home or work, school or library: you can access
    your lists from your own devices or from public computers. With Urlist you’ll never need to backup your browser’s favorite again: all your links are saved and always accessible online.

    by like-minded people

    Every day teachers, designers, developers,
    and Internet addicts, collect their best resources using Urlist. Discover what they are sharing, follow like-minded people to build your network
