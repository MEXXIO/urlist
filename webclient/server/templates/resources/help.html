Urlist Basics

Wonder how does Urlist work?

We prepared a quick introduction to our key features, if you experience any problem with the system just write an email to support@urli.st
1. Lists

A List is a collection of resources around a topic.
You can make a list of the best vegan recipes you found, or for collecting all the sources of a blog post you’re working on. You can invite other people to add links on your lists and share any list on Urlist by email.

2. Links

You can add links from the text field on the list page, or quickly save a web page while browsing, installing our Urlist Button on your browser favorites bar.

Get the Urlist Button here http://urli.st/resources/urlist-button

3. Play the List

A list can be “played” like a slideshow, so you can navigate through its links moving backward and forward.

4. Add a List to your Library

When you add a list, it will appear in your Library; this way you can always keep track of the interesting content you will discover on Urlist.

5. Relist a Link

Any link on Urlist can be relisted. If you find an interesting link on someone else’s list use the relist to save it in one of your lists.
