Urlist Button

Save links while browsing

Once installed the Urlist button allows you to save links in your lists right away, while you're browsing the Internet. Just click on the button: a pop-up window will appear where you can edit all the details of the link or even start a new list.

Install the Urlist button

Chrome

Click on the menu on the top right corner of the browser’s window, go to Settings and on “Appearance” control that “Always show the bookmarks bar” is checked.
Then drag the green button on your bookmarks bar: done!

Firefox

If you don’t see your bookmarks toolbar go to View > Toolsbars > Bookmarks Toolbar
Then drag the green button on your bookmarks bar: done!
