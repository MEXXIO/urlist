About us

Why Urlist?

On the Internet the URL—the “link”—represents the basic element of information, the unique address given to identify a web resource. We are fascinated by that concept, and Urlist is our effort to create a consistent system around it.

When you are collecting your web resources in a list, you are not just saving them for later.
You are giving back to the Web a new URL. Every single list in Urlist has its very own URL (or link) so other people in the Internet can enjoy your hand selected collection of links. Guess what, every single item in a list has its short link too. It's like a child of the list's URL! (For example, urli.st/KTZ/qQC is an item of the list urli.st/KTZ).

We are very ambitious: our plan is to build the largest library of links, giving our users a simple, handy tool to stay close to the information they care about.

Because we love the Web.
