Content Strategist intern (summer project)

Everyone is an expert in their own way. Maybe you know everything about the theories of the Dyson Sphere, or you’re so addicted to Oreo that you could write The Oreology Manifesto. You’re a hijab fashionista who can name more than sixty websites for buying the coolest veils ever, or you have the perfect guide on Earth ovens and biomass cooking stoves.

No matter what you’re interested in, you and everyone else that’s on the internet has valuable knowledge about something they love.

Urlist is building the largest links library on the Web, encouraging any enthusiast, either a teacher, a designer, a developer, even lolcat lovers, to collect and share their knowledge about the most hidden corners of the Internet.

We believe that humans are far better curators than search engines, and that having a point of view on a particular topic is much more valuable than a bunch of SEO-organised links in the top search results.

We are looking for a list maniac, an avid reader, and an internet addict with strong opinions on the most diverse topics - from politics to lolcats, from art to startups. You will contribute to building our Discovery section, driving the creation of UGC, promoting the best lists, and engaging our community. You should be willing to plan and test your ideas, and measure the results (with our help setting up the process).

High proficiency in written English is required.

As Content Strategist of Urlist, you have the opportunity to join a funded startup, located in the heart of Berlin with headquarters at the Factory, surrounded by the best international startups in the city. We are a team of four hardcore developers and awesome designers working hard to make web discovery fun again.

Make a list, attach 300 characters on why you’re the perfect match for this position, and send both to us at: jobs@urli.st
